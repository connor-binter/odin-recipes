Buttered Noodles

Home

Description

Want a simple and quick dinner? Nothing beats buttered noodles!

Ingredients

  • Noodles of choice
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water
  2. Put stove on high, and add a pinch of salt to the water as it is brought to a boil.
  3. Add noodles, and boil for 10-20 minutes or until there is no white in the middle of the noodles.
  4. Drain noodles, and then add back into pot with butter and stir.
