Hawaiian Pizza

Home

Description

Hawaiian pizza is a controversial, yet delicious pie tradition. What follows is a savoury and sweet treat sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • Pre-made pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Canned or fresh pineapple
  • Precooked or raw ham
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Cook and/or slice the ham.
  3. Slice the pineapple to desired size.
  4. Apply sauce to pre-made pizza base, and apply a slight amount of cheese.
  5. Add the toppings, and then cover with a little more cheese to desired amount.
  6. Add the pizza into the oven for 10-20 minutes, or until cheese starts to have golden streaks.
