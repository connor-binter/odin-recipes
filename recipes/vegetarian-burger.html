Vegetarian Burger

Home

Description

Crave a burger without the greasiness of meat? Try a vegetarian burger!

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Toppings and condiments of choice
  • Either a beyond burger or an impossible burger

Steps

  1. Apply oil to pan, and turn on stove heat to medium-high. Don't turn it on too high!
  2. Add patty to pan, as well as buns if toasting is desired. Only toast buns slightly.
  3. Flip patty after four minutes, and cook for another four or until patty is hot in the middle.
  4. Add cheese onto patty. With a pan lid ready, apply a very small splash of water and then cover until cheese is melted.
  5. Add patty to bun and then add any desired toppings!
